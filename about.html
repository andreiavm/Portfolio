how it started:

I was born and grew up in Romania in a small mountain town called Busteni, and I always had an affinity for design, technology, and people. After finishing school, I moved to Bucharest, the capital city of Romania, where I took different freelance jobs in the creative field. Here is where I understood my intrinsic motivation to improve people's lives by building engaging and meaningful experiences. Soon I realized that all the skills and knowledge I have gained so far could be leveraged and I decided to move to the Netherlands to further strengthen my potential and pursue my passion for experience design.

how it's going:

Currently I am living in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, and I am studying User Experience Design as a Bachelor of Science at The Hague University. I am learning new and exciting things about design every day and I am looking for opportunities where I could practice my problem-solving skills, by helping you bridge the gap between business goals and human needs.
