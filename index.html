About

I am a student at The Hague University, studying UX Design. I am interested in pursuing a career as a UX engineer, being responsible for research, ideation, design, development, and implementation. Inspired by the Bauhaus school, I have a modern approach to design, aiming for simplicity, functionality and emotional value.I am proficient in research, ideation, design, prototyping, coding, and conversion rate optimisation. My passions are generative art, sound design, collecting music, graphic design and motion design.
