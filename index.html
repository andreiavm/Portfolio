About

Currently I am a student at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, studying User Experience Design, and I am interested in pursuing a career as a UX engineer, being responsible for research, ideation, design, development, and implementation. Taking inspiration from the Bauhaus school and international typography, I have a modern approach to design, aiming for simplicity and functionality.I consider myself proficient in wireframing, prototyping, coding, colour theory, and design principles, and I am working on developing my skills in user research and motion graphics.
