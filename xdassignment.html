Interface Yourself

my role: design and prototype

project type: university assignment

This is an application I built for university. The scope of this application was to introduce myself to my colleagues, and to give them a clear understanding of my personality, hobbies and vision. I used a more unconventional and artistic approach in order to practice my skills in design and prototyping.

view prototype

aesthetics:

When building this app I focused mostly on the aesthetic choices, and after browsing the web for inspiration, I decided to go forward with a nostalgic user interface vibe, with a modern hipster touch.

Althought now I am an avid Mac user, Windows 98 and Windows XP are the operating systems I grew up with and this vaporware aesthetic is what really sparked my interest in user interface design as a child.

When designing this app, I gave it a slight brutalist twist by mixing together more fonts that don't really go together perfectly, thus creating a pleasant discordance in the eye of the users.

micro-interactions:

A part of the app in which I put extra effort was the interactivity. I found this to be a challenging topic, given the technological constraints of prototyping tools.

Many of the micro-interactions, like the art creator and the music player, took me hours of experimentation and tweaking.

In the end, by designing this app, I have gained valuable experience in building mobile prototypes and I got a better understanding of mobile UI layouts and digital prototyping tools.
